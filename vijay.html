vijay

Career: Began his career as a child artist in 1984 before taking on lead roles in the early 1990s. He gained immense popularity in the mid-1990s and has since starred in numerous blockbuster films, including Ghilli (2004), Thuppakki (2012), Mersal (2017), and Leo (2023). "Thalapathy": His massive, global fanbase affectionately refers to him as "Thalapathy," a Tamil word meaning "commander".
